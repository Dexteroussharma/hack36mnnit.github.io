Hack 36 - MNNIT's Hackathon

Hack 36 is the first edition of Hackathon here at MNNIT Allahabad being organised under the aegis of Computer Science and Engineering Department. It will bring together budding hackers and developers for a feast of 36 continuous hours where they will code solutions to real life problems. Come, be a Hack36.
